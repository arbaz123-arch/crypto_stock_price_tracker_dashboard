About CryptoDash

CryptoDash is a lightweight, beginner-friendly real-time crypto price dashboard built for demo and portfolio use. It fetches data from CoinGecko, shows price charts, favorites persisted in localStorage, and a USD → INR converter.

Features

  • Search coins (CoinGecko)
  • Current price, market cap, 24h change
  • 7-day price chart (Chart.js)
  • Favorites saved locally
  • Dark mode & responsive layout
